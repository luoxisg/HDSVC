Singapore's Unique Challenges

1. Exponential Compute Density

The proliferation of AI/ML workloads is causing exponential growth in rack power density (>50kW), exceeding the thermal dissipation limits of conventional air-cooling architectures.

2. Ambient Thermodynamic Constraints

Singapore's tropical, high-humidity climate makes facility cooling (PUE) a primary energy load, posing a significant barrier to achieving national Green Data Center objectives.

3. Spatiotemporal Constraints

Limited land and high construction costs necessitate solutions with high spatial efficiency, rapid deployment velocity, and superior operational economics.

Tech Deep Dive

Zenith-Cool: A Paradigmatic Shift in Tropical Thermal Management

Our core is the 'Tropic-Hybrid' cooling system. It dynamically coordinates multiple thermal technologies for optimal efficiency across all load scenarios.

1. Biphasic Thermosyphon Economization (XFreecooling)

Leverages patented pneumatic heat-pipe (biphasic thermosyphon) technology to maximize natural economization, achieving 40-70% in cooling energy savings. An AI algorithm intelligently manages operating modes for year-round optimization.

2. Full-Chain Direct Liquid Cooling (DLC) On-Demand

For >50kW AI racks, we seamlessly integrate cold-plate or immersion DLC units. We possess full-chain IP from quick-connectors and CDUs to cold plates, supporting ultra-high thermal densities up to 200kW per rack.

R&D Proposal: The "Tropic-AI" Micro-Module

We are seeking (VC/Government) support to launch the 'Tropic-AI' Micro-Module R&D project. This project aims to optimize and validate our 'Zenith' platform for the Singaporean climate, setting a new global benchmark for tropical data center efficiency.

Key Project Deliverables (KPIs)

KPI 1: Sub-1.15 PUE Validation

Achieve and validate a full-year, real-world PUE of less than 1.15 in a live Singapore (ASHRAE Tropical) environment.

KPI 2: 200kW+ Rack Density Validation

Develop and certify a commercially viable 200kW per-rack full Direct Liquid Cooling (DLC) solution, future-proofed for next-gen AI accelerators.

KPI 3: Heterogeneous Cooling Platform

Deliver an advanced, hardware-agnostic AIOps platform capable of unifying the management and optimization of heterogeneous cooling (air, liquid, hybrid) environments.
